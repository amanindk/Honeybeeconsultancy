About Us

HoneyBee Consultancy is an approved manpower supply agency from the Ministry of External Affairs of India with a valid broad no. ........... l. We have an in-house capacity of 100 to 200 recruitments per month from our head office in Chandigarh. We have tie-ups and business associates in Mumbai, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and other trade center cities across India from where we arrange skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled workers who are ready for license deployment. Our mission is to deliver the best quality manpower because we believe in quality rather than quantity and our vision is to be the leading recruiting agency by building long-term partnerships with employers and employees because client satisfaction is our top priority.

WHY CHOOSE US

Our recruitment process is designed to be flexible and tailored to the specific needs of each employer. Whether an organization is seeking to fill a single position or multiple vacancies, we can provide a range of services to meet their needs, including candidate sourcing, pre-screening, and interviewing. By working with our organization, employers can benefit from our extensive network of job seekers and our expertise in identifying the most qualified candidates for the job. We are committed to providing comprehensive support throughout the recruitment process, ensuring that our clients can focus on their core business activities while we handle the recruitment process on their behalf. At HoneyBee Consultant, we are committed to operating ethically and in compliance with the Emigration Act of 1983. Our agency is registered with the Protector General of Emigrants and we strictly adhere to the guidelines and regulations outlined in the act. We understand the importance of protecting the interests of Indian workers who are seeking employment opportunities overseas and we are dedicated to the welfare of our clients who are already employed, which is why we prioritize their welfare and provide assistance to our clients in the event of unforeseen circumstances and to help we ensure that they can maintain a good quality of life while working abroad by assisting them with any needs that may arise during their employment abroad.
